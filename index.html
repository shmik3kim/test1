Buying Nostalgia

Black Friday 2019. Cyber Monday 2019. Every year I fall into a trap of hyping up this weekend for shopping. It ends up being more stress than should be associated with something that should be fun.

  • I could have bought a UCS Snowspeeder
  • I could have bought a Lego Voltron
  • I could have bought a Lego Batman 1989 Batmobile

But I didn't. I kept asking myself:

  • "What am I going to do with it after I build it?"
  • "Where am I going to put it?"
  • "Am I going to wait till Aaron grows up for us to play with it together?"
  • "Can't I just buy one later?"

In the end... it didn't make a lot of sense to spend $600 on 3 lego sets that I would probably never do anything with... for years. And I have so many other amazing and seemingly essential sets that I haven't touched or seen in years and years (perhaps more than a decade?). So... long term... it probably won't matter. But hmmm... even now I am tempted.
